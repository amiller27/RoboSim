About RoboSim


The University of Pittsburgh's Robotics and Automation Society often creates hardware controlled through python, but had no simple solution to simulate their prototypes before making them.


RoboSim makes prototyping on Raspberry Pi, or Intel Edison easy by combining a blender powered robotics simulation with an Arduino-like control loop, made possible through the infinitely flexible python language.
